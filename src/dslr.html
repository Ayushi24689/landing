Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a DSLR and a mirrorless camera?

DSLR cameras use a mirror to direct light from the lens to the viewfinder, while mirrorless cameras do not have a mirror, making them more compact and generally faster.

Can I use lenses from my old DSLR with a new model?

In most cases, you can use lenses from your old DSLR with a new model if they are from the same brand and compatible with the new camera body.
