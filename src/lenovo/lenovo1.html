Frequently Asked Questions

What is the battery life of the Lenovo IdeaPad 3?

The Lenovo IdeaPad 3 offers up to 7.5 hours of battery life on a full charge, depending on usage and settings.

Does the Lenovo IdeaPad 3 come with a warranty?

Yes, it comes with a 1-year limited warranty, which covers manufacturing defects and hardware issues.

Can I upgrade the RAM on the Lenovo IdeaPad 3?

Yes, the RAM on the Lenovo IdeaPad 3 is upgradeable. It supports up to 12GB of DDR4 RAM.

What ports are available on the Lenovo IdeaPad 3?

The laptop features two USB 3.1 Type-A ports, one USB-C port, an HDMI port, a headphone jack, and an SD card reader.

Is the Lenovo IdeaPad 3 compatible with external displays?

Yes, the IdeaPad 3 supports external displays through its HDMI port and USB-C port with DisplayPort support.
