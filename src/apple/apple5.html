Frequently Asked Questions

What are the key features of AirPods Pro?

AirPods Pro feature Active Noise Cancellation, Transparency mode, and a customizable fit with three sizes of silicone tips. They also offer spatial audio for an immersive listening experience.

How do I pair my AirPods Pro with my device?

To pair your AirPods Pro, open the case near your device and follow the on-screen instructions. They are compatible with iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and other Bluetooth-enabled devices.

How long does the battery last on AirPods Pro?

AirPods Pro offer up to 4.5 hours of listening time with Active Noise Cancellation on and up to 24 hours of listening time with the charging case.

Can I use AirPods Pro with non-Apple devices?

Yes, AirPods Pro can be used with any device that supports Bluetooth audio, although some features like Siri and automatic device switching are limited to Apple devices.

How do I clean my AirPods Pro?

To clean your AirPods Pro, use a soft, dry, lint-free cloth. Do not use liquids or submerge them in water. Clean the silicone ear tips with water and dry them completely before reattaching.
