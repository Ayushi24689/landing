Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the battery life of the iPhone 11?

The iPhone 11 offers up to 17 hours of battery life for video playback and up to 10 hours for internet use.

Does the iPhone 11 support wireless charging?

Yes, the iPhone 11 supports wireless charging with any Qi-compatible charging pad.

Is the iPhone 11 water-resistant?

Yes, the iPhone 11 is rated IP68 for water and dust resistance, meaning it can withstand submersion in up to 2 meters of water for up to 30 minutes.

What is the storage capacity of the iPhone 11?

The iPhone 11 comes in three storage options: 64GB, 128GB, and 256GB.
