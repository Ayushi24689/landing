Frequently Asked Questions

What are the key features of the iPhone 14?

The iPhone 14 features a Super Retina XDR display, A15 Bionic chip, improved camera system, and support for 5G connectivity. It comes in multiple colors and has enhanced durability with a Ceramic Shield front cover.

What is the battery life of the iPhone 14?

The iPhone 14 offers up to 20 hours of video playback or up to 16 hours of streaming video playback on a single charge, depending on usage.

Is the iPhone 14 water-resistant?

Yes, the iPhone 14 is rated IP68 for water and dust resistance, meaning it can withstand submersion in up to 6 meters of water for up to 30 minutes.

Does the iPhone 14 support wireless charging?

Yes, the iPhone 14 supports MagSafe and Qi wireless charging, allowing for convenient and efficient wireless charging.

What colors are available for the iPhone 14?

The iPhone 14 is available in Midnight, Starlight, Product Red, Blue, and Purple.
