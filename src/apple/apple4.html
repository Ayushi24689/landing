Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty period for the iPad Pro?

The iPad Pro comes with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. You can also purchase AppleCare+ for extended coverage.

Is the iPad Pro water-resistant?

No, the iPad Pro is not water-resistant. It is recommended to keep it away from liquids to avoid damage.

What accessories are compatible with the iPad Pro?

The iPad Pro is compatible with various accessories including the Apple Pencil, Magic Keyboard, and Smart Keyboard Folio. Check for compatibility before purchasing.

Can I use my iPad Pro with external displays?

Yes, you can connect your iPad Pro to external displays using a USB-C to HDMI or USB-C to DisplayPort adapter, depending on your display's input.
