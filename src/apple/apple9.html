Frequently Asked Questions

What is the iPhone 15's battery life?

The iPhone 15 offers up to 20 hours of video playback, making it ideal for all-day use without needing to recharge frequently.

Does the iPhone 15 support wireless charging?

Yes, the iPhone 15 supports MagSafe wireless charging up to 15W, as well as Qi wireless charging.

Is the iPhone 15 waterproof?

The iPhone 15 is rated IP68 for water and dust resistance, meaning it can withstand submersion in up to 6 meters of water for up to 30 minutes.

What storage options are available for the iPhone 15?

The iPhone 15 is available in 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB storage options to fit your needs.
