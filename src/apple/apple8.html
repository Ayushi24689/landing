Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the main features of the iPhone 15 Pro?

The iPhone 15 Pro features a 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display, a powerful A17 Bionic chip, a triple-camera system with enhanced low-light performance, and a sleek titanium and ceramic shield design.

2. How long is the warranty for the iPhone 15 Pro?

The iPhone 15 Pro comes with a standard 1-year warranty, covering hardware defects and manufacturing issues. AppleCare+ is available for extended coverage.

3. Is the iPhone 15 Pro waterproof?

Yes, the iPhone 15 Pro has an IP68 rating for water and dust resistance, meaning it can withstand submersion in up to 6 meters of water for up to 30 minutes.

4. What accessories are compatible with the iPhone 15 Pro?

The iPhone 15 Pro is compatible with MagSafe accessories, including cases, chargers, and wallets. It also supports Lightning accessories and wireless charging.

5. Can I transfer data from my old iPhone to the iPhone 15 Pro?

Yes, you can transfer data from your old iPhone to the iPhone 15 Pro using the Quick Start feature during setup. You can also use iCloud or iTunes backups to transfer your data.
