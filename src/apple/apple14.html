Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the battery life of the iPhone 10?

A: The iPhone 10 offers up to 14 hours of talk time and up to 12 hours of internet use.

Q: Does the iPhone 10 support wireless charging?

A: Yes, the iPhone 10 supports wireless charging using Qi-compatible chargers.

Q: Is the iPhone 10 water-resistant?

A: The iPhone 10 is water-resistant with an IP67 rating, meaning it can withstand being submerged in up to 1 meter of water for up to 30 minutes.

Q: What storage options are available for the iPhone 10?

A: The iPhone 10 is available in 64GB and 256GB storage options.

Q: Does the iPhone 10 have a headphone jack?

A: No, the iPhone 10 does not have a headphone jack. It supports audio through the Lightning port or via Bluetooth.
