What is the battery life of the Xiaomi Poco X3 Pro?

The Xiaomi Poco X3 Pro comes with a 5160mAh battery, providing up to 5-7 hours of screen-on time depending on usage.

The Xiaomi Poco X3 Pro is available in two storage options: 128GB and 256GB, expandable via microSD card.
