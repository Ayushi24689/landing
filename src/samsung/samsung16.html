Frequently Asked Questions

What is the battery life of the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1?

The battery life of the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1 is approximately 10-12 hours with regular use. It has a 4380mAh battery that supports fast charging.

Does the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1 support wireless charging?

Yes, the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1 supports wireless charging as well as fast wireless charging. It also supports reverse wireless charging, allowing you to charge other devices using your phone.

Can I use the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1 while it's folded?

Yes, you can use the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1 while it's folded. The outer display allows for full functionality even when the device is closed.

What kind of warranty comes with the Samsung Galaxy Fold 1?

The Samsung Galaxy Fold 1 typically comes with a one-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. For more details, please check with the retailer or Samsung's warranty policy.
