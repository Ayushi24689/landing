Frequently Asked Questions

What is the battery life of the Samsung Galaxy Fold?

The Samsung Galaxy Fold has a battery life of up to 24 hours with typical usage. Battery life may vary depending on usage and settings.

Does the Samsung Galaxy Fold support 5G?

Yes, the Samsung Galaxy Fold supports 5G connectivity, allowing for faster internet speeds and improved connectivity.

Is the Samsung Galaxy Fold water-resistant?

The Samsung Galaxy Fold is not water-resistant. It is recommended to keep the device away from water and moisture.

Can I use the Samsung Galaxy Fold with one hand?

While the Samsung Galaxy Fold is designed for a two-handed experience when using the main display, the cover display allows for one-handed use when the device is folded.

What is the warranty period for the Samsung Galaxy Fold?

The Samsung Galaxy Fold comes with a standard 1-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects and hardware issues. Additional coverage options may be available.
