Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my tripod?

It is recommended to clean your tripod after each use, especially if it was exposed to dust or moisture.

What should I do if a part of my tripod breaks?

If a part of your tripod breaks, consult the manufacturer for replacement parts or consider a repair kit.

Can I use regular cleaning products on my tripod?

It is best to use products specifically designed for camera equipment to avoid damage.
